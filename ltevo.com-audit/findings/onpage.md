# On-Page SEO Audit Findings

## Score: 92/100

## What Works Well
- ✅ All crawled pages have a defined title tag.
- ✅ All crawled pages have a meta description tag.
- ✅ All crawled pages have exactly one H1 tag.

## Identified Issues & Areas for Improvement

### 1. Sub-optimal title tag length
- **Severity:** `Medium`
- **Description:** Found 2 titles below 30 characters (e.g. 'Términos de Uso | LTEvo' on https://ltevo.com/terminos). 
- **Recommendation:** Optimize title tags to be between 30 and 60 characters to ensure they display fully in search engine results without truncation.

### 2. Sub-optimal meta description length
- **Severity:** `Low`
- **Description:** Found 3 short (< 120 chars) and 1 long (> 160 chars) meta descriptions.
- **Recommendation:** Rewrite meta descriptions to fit in the 120-160 character range.
